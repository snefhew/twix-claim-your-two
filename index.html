User Journey Map

The AIDA Funnel

Grab the scroll-stopper

Max is bored during a loading screen. He picks up his phone for a quick dopamine hit. We need to stop his thumb in under 2 seconds.

Touchpoint

TikTok & Instagram Reels

Mobile-first, vertical video. Appears natively in the feed between organic content — no pre-roll, no skip button.

Emotion

Surprise & Delight

Not loud or aggressive — just unexpectedly joyful. A visual glitch that makes you smile before you even know it's an ad.

Archetype: The Jester

Content

"The Glitch" — AI-Generated Double Moments

Short AI-generated videos (Runway Gen-2) of relatable small wins: catching the bus, pulling the perfect espresso, landing a trick. The moment glitches playfully, time rewinds, and it happens again — with joyful sparkles and confetti. Ends with the sonic "Glitch" sound and: "Why celebrate once? Twix. Two is more than one."

Speak his language

Max is watching a stream or YouTube on his second screen. He's engaged but open to entertainment that fits his world.

Touchpoint

Twitch Overlay & YouTube Mid-rolls

Native placements that feel part of the stream, not interruptions. Triggered by in-game events (Double Kill, win streak) for maximum relevance.

Emotion

Recognition & Belonging

"This brand gets me." The content uses gaming language, understands the culture, and shows up at the right moment.

Archetype: The Jester

Content

In-Stream Double Win Activations

When a streamer scores a double achievement, a playful Twix animation triggers with our signature "Glitch" sound: "A good thing, twice. Just like your Twix." On YouTube: short, punchy mid-rolls — fast, fun, zero cringe.

Make him crave it

It's 3PM. Max hasn't paused in hours. His energy is dipping. He deserves a reward — and we're about to make him feel it.

Touchpoint

Spotify Audio & Instagram Feed

Audio ads between songs during focus playlists. Visual macro-content in the Instagram feed — scroll-stopping product photography.

Emotion

Pure Sensory Craving

No sales pitch. Just the sound of chocolate cracking and caramel stretching. Innocent, warm indulgence.

Archetype: The Jester + Hedonism

Content

ASMR Hyper-Foley & Macro Visuals

On Spotify: a warm voice with exaggerated foley — the snap of biscuit, the stretch of caramel — times two. "Hear that crunch? That's one. And because it's Twix... here's number two." On Instagram: AI-generated hyperrealistic macro photography of glistening chocolate and golden caramel, always in pairs.

Make it effortless

Max wants that Twix. Right now. Without leaving his chair. Remove every barrier between craving and snacking.

Touchpoint

Delivery Apps & Digital OOH

Push notifications via Flink, Gorillas, UberEats timed to the afternoon dip. Digital billboards at transit hubs and supermarket entrances.

Emotion

Instant Gratification

Zero friction, maximum reward. A playful nudge, not a hard sell. Confirming what Max already knows: two is the way.

Archetype: The Jester

Content

Data-Driven Dynamic Ads

AI-powered timing: ads deploy exactly when our persona's energy dips (data-driven media buying). Push notification at 3:15PM: "Your afternoon just got an upgrade. Twice. Add Twix — delivered in 10 min." At DOOH screens: two Twix bars appearing playfully from nothing.

Sonic Identity

The Dopamine Drop

Every touchpoint has a sound. This is how Twix sounds — playful, energetic, and unmistakably double.

Mood

Playful & Energetic

A joyful burst of dopamine — like an unexpected surprise during a boring afternoon. Never aggressive, always warm.

Tempo

130 BPM / Dynamic

Fast and bouncy with sudden drops of silence before the punchline. The pause makes the payoff bigger.

Instruments

Electronic & Foley

Bouncy synths and 808s mixed with exaggerated real-world sounds — the snap of chocolate, the stretch of caramel.

Signature Sound

The Glitch

A short digital stutter right before the second Twix appears. The audible confirmation: here comes number two.

Sonic Logo Variations

Variation 1 — The Original Drop

Full energy: synth build → glitch → bass drop + snap

Variation 2 — The Minimal Glitch

Stripped back: just the glitch stutter + clean resolve

Variation 3 — The ASMR Snap

Sensory focus: foley-heavy with the chocolate crunch x2
