Sonic Identity

The Dopamine Drop

Every touchpoint has a sound. This is how Twix sounds — playful, energetic, and unmistakably double.

Mood

Playful & Energetic

A joyful burst of dopamine — like an unexpected surprise during a boring afternoon.

Tempo

130 BPM / Dynamic

Fast and bouncy with sudden silence before the punchline. The pause makes the payoff bigger.

Instruments

Electronic & Foley

Bouncy synths and 808s with exaggerated real-world sounds — the snap of chocolate, the stretch of caramel.

Signature

A short digital stutter right before the second Twix appears. The audible confirmation: here comes number two.
