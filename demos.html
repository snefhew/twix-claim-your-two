Scarcity
🟡
"Only 2 left in stock." Creates urgency. Ethical if true, manipulative if fabricated. Cialdini principle #6.
Social Proof
🟢
"1.247 people ordered this today." Reassures through group behavior. Generally ethical when data is real. Cialdini principle #3.
Authority
🟡
"Recommended based on your profile." Uses algorithmic authority. Ethical if transparent about data use. Cialdini principle #5.
Urgency
🔴
"Free delivery ends in 4:59." Countdown timer creates FOMO. Often manipulative since timer resets. Cialdini principle #6 + dark pattern risk.
Reciprocity
🟢
"Free second Twix on first order." Giving something free triggers desire to return the favor. Ethical when the offer is genuine. Cialdini principle #1.
🟢 Ethical · 🟡 Context-dependent · 🔴 Dark pattern risk
